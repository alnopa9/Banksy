This piece is different from Banksy's normal artwork. It isn't a painting or a statue. It is a life-size blow-up doll that has been dressed in prisoner orange and handcuffed with a black hood placed over its face. The doll was placed next to the "Big Thunder Mountain Railroad" at Disneyland in California. The piece refers to the war on terror and to highlight the plight of terror suspects.
